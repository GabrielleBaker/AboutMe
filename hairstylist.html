I have always been a creative person, which led me to my first career as a hairstylist.
During the first three years, I was an apprentice stylist, meaning I gained practical experience on the job, I also worked a second job as a waitress in the evenings.
After this intensive period, I became a hairstylist with my own client base.
I am capable in all areas of hairstyling, from chemical services such as permanent waves, brazillian blowouts and color services, ranging to cutting and styling for any human with hair regardless of gender.
My specialities are in upstyling hair for weddings and special events, hair extension services, and crazy color services.
